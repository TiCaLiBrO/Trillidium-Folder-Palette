# Trillidium Folder Palette
This project allows you to color your folders, allowing you to color-code them to your liking.

![Picture of all 39 folders in all of their glory](https://github.com/TiCaLiBrO/Trillidium-Folder-Palette/blob/main/TrillidiumPalette.png)

If you like what you see and want to color your folders in this way for better organization or for aesthetics, the instructions are below.

## Installation

### Download Folder Painter

First, go [here](https://folder-painter.en.softonic.com/?utm_source=SEM&utm_medium=paid&utm_campaign=EN_CA_GamesForWindows_downloads_tp&gad_source=1&gad_campaignid=22816290447&gclid=Cj0KCQjwrZTRBhDSARIsAHidYfcWmScEkQNYI3eTsphgWTRsVErhu9NY16Jzcd5yw52z9Y5UZ9YVy58aAi44EALw_wcB) to download **Folder Painter** by Sordum for Microsoft Windows.

Sordum has their own color palettes, which are nice, but none of them are as consistent as mine.

### Download Trillidium Folder Palette

Download the files from this repository, then extract them and replace the files in Folder Painter located at FolderPainter/icons.
FolderPainter should be located in Program Files.

- Pack 1 is saturated colors.

- Pack 2 is dark colors.

- Pack 3 is light colors.

The files are yours to edit, rename, or do whatever you want with. Light, dark, and saturated packs can be uninstalled or deleted if you only want one or two of the three packs.

## Additional Details

The color palette is as consistent as possible, consisting of 3 primary colors (Red, Green, Blue), every even mix of them (Yellow, Cyan, Magenta), and every even color between them, giving a total color wheel of 12 colors.
Of the 12 colors, there are three brightnesses available, giving a total of 36 colors.
Then, to top it all off, White, Grey, and Black are added into the Light, Saturated, and Dark packs, respectively, for a total of 39 colors to choose from.
